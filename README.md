# Threadbare — A Life

A generational life simulator you can play on your phone. Tap **Age Up** to live a year at a time. Between years, choose what to do — school, careers, relationships, health, finances, nightlife, crime. When your character dies, pick up the thread through one of their children and continue the bloodline. The **Tree** view shows every life across every generation.

It's one self-contained HTML file with no internet required after it loads, and it saves your progress on your own phone.

## What's inside

**Start of life** — On a new game you get an opening blurb with your character's birthplace (city + country), birthday, and traits. You can edit the first and last name before you begin, and change your name again later in life.

**Careers** across many tracks: trades (mechanic, electrician, plumber, trucker), public service with promotion ladders (police, firefighter, military), corporate ladder up to CEO, medicine (nurse, surgeon, vet), law up to judge, plus long-shots — NBA, pro footballer, **UFC fighter**, **secret agent**, **politician** (with elections), musician, actor, model, author, YouTuber, startup founder. Part-time jobs for teens and students.

**Family** — date, marry, and have children. Natural conception odds fall with age and cap at 47; a **fertility clinic** extends that to 55; **adoption** is available at any adult age (and can bring in an older child). Children age year by year in the background, so when you inherit one they're the right age.

**Money tab** — an **investment portfolio** with low / medium / high risk tiers that grow (or crash) differently each year; **businesses you own** like an online casino, a restaurant, or rental property that pay income; and **luxury** purchases — private island, yacht, helicopter, supercar, luxury RV, fine jewelry. Cars depreciate, houses appreciate, and everything passes down as part of your estate.

**Health & insurance** — buy insurance, see a doctor, go to therapy, enter rehab, get cosmetic surgery. Medical emergencies are survivable but ruinous without insurance. Stress, addiction, and chronic conditions are tracked.

**Life & leisure** — hit the nightlife to meet people, join **social media** and grow a following into brand deals, plus a deep pool of random events for childhood, teens, adulthood, and old age. Pets age, bond, boost your happiness and health, and are mourned when they pass. Crime can land you in prison with a record.

**Interactive choices & consequences** — some years stop and ask you to *decide*: stand up to a bully or report them, take the risky startup job or stay safe, give in to temptation or walk away, restore the old family house or sell it. Many choices plant a seed that pays off — or comes back to bite — years later, so a decision at 16 can echo at 40.

## Put it on GitHub Pages and install it on your phone
1. Upload **all** the files in this folder together — keep them in the same place: `index.html`, `manifest.webmanifest`, `icon-192.png`, `icon-512.png`.
2. In the repo: **Settings → Pages → Build and deployment**, set Source to **Deploy from a branch**, branch `main`, folder `/ (root)`. Save.
3. Wait a minute, then open the Pages URL in **Chrome on Android**.
4. Tap **⋮** → **Add to Home screen**. It installs as an app, opens fullscreen, and saves your bloodline locally.

## Notes
- Saving uses your phone's local storage and works on the installed/hosted version. The **Continue** button appears once a bloodline is saved.
- Old saves from earlier versions are automatically upgraded when you continue them.
- Everything runs on-device. No account, no server, no tracking.
